Short Bio

Songcen Xu received his Bachelor Degree in Wuhan University of Technology in China in 2008, and the M.Sc. degree in communications engineering and the Ph.D. degree in electronic engineering from the University of York, York, U.K., in 2011 and 2015, respectively.

He is currently working as a principal research engineer at Noah' Ark Lab, Huawei technologies, in the area of Artificial Intelligence. Especially in the area of Computer Vision.

Education

  • PhD (October 2011 - July 2015): Major in Electronics Engineering, University of York, supervised by Prof. Rodrigo C. de Lamare .
  • Master (October 2010 - September 2011): Major in Communications Engineering, University of York.
  • Bachelor (September 2004- July 2008): Major in Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering, Wuhan University of Technology.

    Research Interests

    My research mainly focus on Computer Vision and their applications on mobile device.

    Specifically, my current research interests include:

    • 3D Digital Human(including: human reconstruction, performance capture, transfer and generation, neural rendering)
    • 3D environment semantic understanding
    • Multimodal video understanding
    • Image and video retrieval;
    • Object detection and semantic segmentation for image and video
